WHO

ARE

Founded in 2009 in Jackson Hole, WY, Purple Orange LLC produces small batch, high quality brand communications mixed with creativity, modern mediums and a respected authentic voice. Our professional influences range from publishing, large and small agency work, and former in-house marketing; we like to think we see the whole picture for our clients. Specializing in active lifestyle and natural living, our clients have spanned industry-leading performance apparel brands, food and beverage, consumer tech, and non-profit work.

MEANINGFUL COMMUNICATIONS

We Believe Good PR Is About Meaningful Communication.

It’s no longer enough to make a good product. People — especially millennials — want to feel positive connections to the brands and products they use.

We look for ways to distill the complex, create stories that connect, and share them with journalists who trust us.

Climate Neutral is a non-profit organization that independently certifies that all the greenhouse gas required to operate a company are measured and off-set by verified carbon-reduction credits.

Purple Orange set up this feature cover-story with Climate Neutral co-Founders Peak Design and BioLite and celebrity athlete Alex Honnold with the OR DAILY (the official magazine of Outdoor Retailer) to help launch the organization in June of 2019 at the Outdoor Retailer Show.
